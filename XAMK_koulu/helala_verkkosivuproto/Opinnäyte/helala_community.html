The Crew and Fans

The developers of Elite, Frontier Developments, is based out of UK. They have a loyal fanbase made up of tens of thousands of people, people who love the game so much they put their heart and soul to make the world of Elite even more rich in content. The lore is consistently updated and more fiction written every day. Some of this make it to the game, and recently another official fiction package was added to the game. Q&A events, demonstrations, competitions, live audio drama shows, and conventions are held every year and the following is growing. With Elite being the huge success it has been Frontier has promised 10 years worth of content yet to come. Love it or hate it, this game is going to be with us for a long time.
