INTRODUCTION

Elite: Dangerous is a game from Frontier Developments, the ones who brought you Rollercoaster Tycoon 3 and many other world builders. Through a kickstarter in 2012, the developers had the resources to bring back an icon of space simulators from the 80's back to life.

Discover over 400 billion star systems, explore planets up close and take you and your friends to the ends of our Milky Way galaxy. Upcoming expansions promise to make the world of Elite more diverse, more intense and more mysterious.

You command your own ship, which you can purchase and outfit as you see fit in a variety of space platforms and stations. After the launch of Frontiers latest patches, there are now over 30 ships to choose from and still more are coming.

Each ship can be outfitted and customized. Ships vary from simple cargo ships and luxury space vehicles to monstrous battleships, ranging in size from little over a tenniscourt size Sidewinder to a 155m long Anaconda. Expansions promise bigger ships and a more fleshed out purpose for them.
